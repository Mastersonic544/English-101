✍️Writing

Imagine you are an artist. Describe a painting you would create. What colors would you use? What would the painting be about? How would it make people feel?

Write about a sports event you attended or watched. Describe the atmosphere, the players, and the excitement. Would you like to play that sport?

Share a step-by-step guide on how to do your favorite hobby. Include materials needed and the process. Explain why you enjoy this hobby.

Write a short story about a special day with your family. Include who was there, what you did, and why it was memorable.
